About GLANCE

GLANCE, the Mega Alumni Job Fair, is an exceptional opportunity for students of GLA University Mathura to connect with leading recruiters and explore exciting career prospects. Organized by the Department of Alumni Affairs, GLANCE promises to be a gateway to professional growth and success, offering a platform for students to showcase their skills and aspirations.

  • Varied job and internship offers.
  • Interactive events for company insights.
  • Networking with recruiters and peers.
  • Personal and professional growth.
  • Career advancement opportunities.
