About GLANCE

GLANCE, the inaugural job fair hosted by GLA University Mathura, promises students an exciting opportunity to explore diverse career paths and connect with esteemed organizations. With engaging events and networking opportunities, GLANCE is set to empower students in their journey towards professional success.

Benefits for students
  • Varied job and internship offers.
  • Interactive events for company insights.
  • Networking with recruiters and peers.
  • Personal and professional growth.
  • Career advancement opportunities.

Details of The Job Fair


  • Date: 24th-25th April, 2024

  • Time: 09:00 AM onwards

  • Venue: CSED, AB-11, GLA university, Mathura
